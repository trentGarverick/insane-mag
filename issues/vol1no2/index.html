The health workers on the ground in Ituri Province and the border areas of Uganda are conducting contact tracing, sample collection, treatment, and containment operations in conditions that outbreak response specialists describe as "extremely challenging."

They are not a punchline. This section of INSANE is not satirical. The people testing, tracing, treating, and protecting in DRC and Uganda right now are doing one of the harder jobs available to human beings, and they are doing it well.
